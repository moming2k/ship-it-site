About GitResolve

GitResolve closes one specific gap: you have a project sitting in a GitHub or GitLab repository, and you want it live on the web without working through hosting settings, environment configuration, or account setup you don't need yet.

Paste a repository link, and we take you straight into Vercel's own project-import flow with the repository pre-filled. From there, everything happens under your own account — your project deploys to infrastructure you control, not ours.

What GitResolve does

  • Reads the repository link you paste and checks that it points to a real GitHub or GitLab project
  • Builds a pre-filled link into Vercel's own deployment flow
  • Sends you there — deployment itself happens entirely on Vercel's infrastructure, under your account

What GitResolve doesn't do

  • We don't host your project ourselves
  • We don't store, read, or retain your repository's contents
  • We don't require an account, login, or payment on this site
